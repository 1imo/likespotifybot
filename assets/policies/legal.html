Legal — LikeSpotifyBot What this bot does LikeSpotifyBot links your Telegram account to Spotify (OAuth) and, when enabled, saves the track you are listening to after a quick pause → resume gesture. Playback is polled via the Spotify Web API while you are connected. Data we use • Telegram user ID and messages/commands you send to the bot • Spotify account tokens (access and refresh), playback state, and track identifiers needed to save tracks • Analytics events (e.g. commands, gestures, errors) as configured by the operator Tokens are stored securely by the service operator. Do not share your Telegram account or Spotify login with others. Third parties • Telegram — messaging platform • Spotify — music service; subject to Spotify’s terms and privacy policy Your choices • Disconnect Spotify from /start at any time • Use /toggle to disable gesture detection without disconnecting • Stop using the bot and revoke the app under your Spotify account settings Disclaimer This bot is provided as-is, without warranty. The operator is not affiliated with Spotify or Telegram. Saving tracks modifies your Spotify library; you are responsible for how you use the bot.
