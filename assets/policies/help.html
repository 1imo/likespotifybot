Help — LikeSpotifyBot Commands /start — Connect Spotify or manage your account /toggle — Enable or disable quick pause → like gesture /help — This page /about — About this bot /legal — Terms and privacy How to save a track While Spotify is playing, pause (playback stops) and resume within about four seconds. The bot saves the current track to Your Library. A brief tap that Spotify reports as paused works best. Gestures are ignored during the first 2.5 seconds of each track (e.g. right after a skip or when a song starts), so starting playback or changing tracks won’t trigger a save. Requirements Spotify must be active (app or device). The bot polls playback while you are connected.
